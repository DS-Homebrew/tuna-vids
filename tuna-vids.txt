Tuna-viDS by Chishm

Controls:
B - Play/Pause
X - Toggle bottom backlight
Left - increase video delay by one frame
Right - decrease video delay by one frame

Instructions:
1) Copy tuna-viDS.nds to the root of your flash card or to
   /DS/tuna-viDS/tuna-viDS.nds or to
   /data/tuna-viDS/tuna-viDS.nds
2) DLDI patch tuna-open.nds and copy it to your flash card
3) Run tuna-open.nds
4) Select an AVI file to play

Converting Videos:
The following command can be used to encode a video for playback
using Tuna-viDS. You will need FFmpeg with libmp3lame and mpeg4
encoding support compiled in.

ffmpeg -i <input-file> -f avi -r 12 -s 256x192 -b 192k -bt 64k \
	-vcodec mpeg4 -deinterlace \
	-acodec libmp3lame -ar 32000 -ab 128k -ac 2 tuna-vids.avi
